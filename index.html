Welcome to Mei Rose Connor's Website

Hi, I'm Mei Rose, an independent scholar of Formal Logic, a web developer, and a cat lover who owns no cats.

I studied Mathematics and Linguistics in my undergraduate degree program at Stony Brook University, and have now shifted my focus towards finding ways of sharing the joy of Mathematics and Formal Logic with students of all ages through interactive software applications. I like to find the intersections between my interests and the creative arts, and yes, it's true, I love cats but own none.

This site is full of projects in progress, projects completed, and other things I've made or are working on for fun. Feel free to navigate between section using the buttons provided at the top and bottom of every section.

Projects, Current and Past.

Current Projects

Magic 15 Python Game

Compete against another player to select numbers to add to 15 before they do! This project really showed me how much we take for granted in modern web development...

Kalish-Montague-Mar LaTeX Formatter

Ever wanted to see your natural deductions transformed from the way that we write them on the board to a nice LaTeX format? I did. So I started this project.

Admintopia

This satirical clicker game simulates a world called Admintopia, where the only thing that university student tuition can go towards is hiring more administrators.
